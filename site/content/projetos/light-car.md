## Dog & quote

**URL:** [https://dogandquote.netlify.com/](https://dogandquote.netlify.com/)


## Tecnologias utilizadas

* HTML5
* CSS3 (css-grid, flexbox e css-animations)
* SVG 
* Javascript
* Netlify
